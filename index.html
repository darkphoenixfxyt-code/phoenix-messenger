v1.2 to v1.4 Features

Phoenix Messenger already covers the core communication stack across chat, communities, calls, sharing, and polish.

Friends and DMs

Add friends, accept requests, start direct messages, and keep conversations synced across refreshes.

Servers and Channels

Build communities with text channels, voice channels, roles, invites, moderation, and server settings.

Voice and Video Calls

LiveKit powers voice calls, video calls, speaking indicators, reconnects, and better call states.

Screen Sharing

Share your screen in calls and voice spaces with improved fullscreen viewing and multi-view layouts.

Media Sharing

Cloudinary uploads support images, attachments, and a smoother media workflow inside the messenger.

Message Power Tools

Replies, reactions, edit, delete, pinned messages, themes, and mobile-friendly layouts are built in.
